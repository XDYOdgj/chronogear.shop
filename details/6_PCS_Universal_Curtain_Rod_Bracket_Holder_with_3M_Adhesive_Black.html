6-Piece Universal Curtain Rod Brackets with 3M Adhesive

Upgrade your space with these 6-piece universal curtain rod brackets, built for easy, secure, and damage-free installation. Designed to support a wide range of rod diameters, from 0.6'' to 1.5'', they are the perfect solution for enhancing your bathroom, kitchen, or living room decor.

Each bracket features a powerful 3M adhesive backing, capable of holding up to 14 pounds, while the option to attach using screws increases the weight capacity to 20 pounds. This ensures your curtains or towel rods stay firmly in place, whether you want a clean, sleek look or a more durable setup.

The versatile design of these brackets makes them compatible with smooth surfaces like glass, tile, marble, and stainless steel. Their lightweight yet sturdy construction ensures long-term durability and resistance to wear and tear, making them ideal for both residential and commercial use.

Whether you're a DIY enthusiast or looking for a hassle-free upgrade, these brackets offer a modern, stylish way to install curtain rods. They are a must-have for anyone wanting to enhance functionality without compromising on aesthetics.
